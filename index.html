I worked in the accounting field for seventeen years specializing in accounts receivable, cash management, account reconciliations, payroll, billing, collections, system creation and enhancements. While striving to improve my skills on the job, I began automating all my areas of responsibility. This resulted in the automation of many processes being performed throughout the company. This eventually led to me filling the role of Project Manager for our IT Department. However my true passion was always the automation of the processes, and not necessarily project management. I found myself taking a giant leap, leaving behind a place I felt I would eventually retire from and taking a job as a Jr developer with very little exposure/experience in coding.

In September 2017 I was hired at American Title. It was truly a great environment for my continued growth and learning. While I was able to complete smaller stories, I was still missing some of the basics and fundamentals I felt necessary to make me whole, I really needed to know more. It was at that time I decided to leave American Title and signed up at Tech Academy. I dove head first into their software development bootcamp. The course was 40+ study hours per week for 16 weeks covering C#, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. Unfortunately, entering into this fulltime commitment required me to take some time off.

Which brings us to today. I have completed my training and look forward to the opportunity to work with a quality organization in a field I have worked very hard to prepare for. I have and always will continue to work diligently strengthening my skills and improving my capabilities in every way possible. I hope to partner with a company that can benefit from my abilities and one that will offer me an opportunity to grow on the job.
